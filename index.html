Our Mission

At Orphan Prosperity, we are committed to transforming the lives of vulnerable orphans in Afghanistan. Through education, skill-building, and care, we aim to provide every child with the opportunity to thrive and succeed.

Why We Exist

Afghanistan faces many challenges, and children often bear the brunt of them. Many are left without families, resources, or hope. Orphan Prosperity exists to bridge that gap, ensuring every child we care for receives the love, education, and opportunities they deserve.
